The Aston Martin DB12 is a grand touring car produced by the British car manufacturer Aston Martin.
The car was unveiled in May 2023 as a replacement for the DB11.

The DB12 was presented on 24 May 2023,[3][4] at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival.[5]
The DB12 is also the first Aston Martin vehicle to wear the brand's new logo.[6][7]
